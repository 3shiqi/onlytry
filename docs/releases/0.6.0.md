# Release 0.6.0

Date: 2026-04-27

## Summary

Rebuilt the Calendar tab into a persistent monthly grid that combines real training history, logged external sport, and future projected prescriptions.

## Included

- new Dexie persistence layer in `src/trainingDb.js`
- persisted `TRAIN` history logging from the workout executor
- persisted `PLAY` activity logging from the play logger
- monthly-grid `CalendarPage` with day selection and detail readout
- future planning with explicit rest-day cadence

## Behavioral Notes

- historical calendar days now come from IndexedDB rather than transient React state alone
- today can render as a completed real-world log day while tomorrow and beyond remain algorithmically projected
- `REST (休息)` days are visually lighter than planned recovery prescriptions
- `currentTSS` still remains the main driver for future projection tone and ordering
